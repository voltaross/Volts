Requester Pro v2.0.6

Automatic Spotify & Twitch integration. Simple setup, zero tokens.

Download v2.0.6

1 Setup Spotify API

Go to the Spotify Developer Dashboard and create a new App.

  • Add http://127.0.0.1:8888/callback to Redirect URIs.
  • Save and copy your Client ID and Secret.

2 Launch & Link

Open RequesterPro.exe and follow the buttons in order:

  1. Save Settings: Paste Spotify keys.
  2. Connect Twitch: Click "Authorize" in the popup.
  3. Link Spotify: Grant playback control.
