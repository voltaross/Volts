Requester Pro v2.0.4

Automatic Spotify & Twitch integration. Simple setup, zero tokens.

Download v2.0.4

Now featuring One-Click Twitch Auth

1 Setup Spotify API

Go to the Spotify Developer Dashboard and create a new App.

  • Click Edit Settings.
  • Add http://127.0.0.1:8888/callback to the Redirect URIs.
  • Save and copy your Client ID and Client Secret.

2 Create the Reward

In your Twitch Dashboard, create a Channel Point Reward named exactly Song Request.

IMPORTANT: You must toggle "Require Viewer to Enter Text" to ON. This allows the app to read the song titles from your chat!

3 Link & Launch

Open RequesterPro.exe and follow the buttons in order. No manual tokens required!

  1. Save Settings: Paste your Spotify ID/Secret and save.
  2. Connect Twitch: A browser window will open. Click "Authorize" to link your channel.
  3. Link Spotify: Authorize the app to control your music playback.

4 Stream Features

v2.0.4 Enhancements

  • Auto-Announce: The app automatically posts the song name in your Twitch chat when it starts playing.
  • Smart Requests: Supports both text searches and direct Spotify links.
  • One-Click Auth: No more copying and pasting long Twitch tokens.

5 Add to OBS

Add a Browser Source in OBS:

  • URL: http://127.0.0.1:8888/overlay
  • Size: 400px x 150px
