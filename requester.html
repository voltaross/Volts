Requester Pro v2.0.4

Automatic Spotify & Twitch integration. Simple setup, zero tokens.

Download v2.0.4

Now featuring One-Click Twitch Auth

1 Setup Spotify API

Go to the Spotify Developer Dashboard and create a new App.

  • Click Edit Settings.
  • Add http://127.0.0.1:8888/callback to the Redirect URIs.
  • Save and copy your Client ID and Client Secret.

2 Create the Reward

In your Twitch Dashboard, create a Channel Point Reward named exactly Song Request.

IMPORTANT: You must toggle "Require Viewer to Enter Text" to ON. This allows the app to read song names from chat.

3 Link & Launch

Open RequesterPro.exe and follow the buttons in order. No manual tokens required!

  1. Save Settings: Paste your Spotify ID/Secret and save.
  2. Connect Twitch: An authorize window will open. Click "Authorize".
  3. Link Spotify: Opens your browser to control music playback.

4 Auto-Announcements

v2.0.4 Features

  • Chat Announcement: The app posts the song name in your Twitch chat automatically when it starts.
  • Auto-Updater: Get a choice to update or skip new versions instantly.
