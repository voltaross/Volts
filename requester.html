Requester Pro v2.0.6

Automatic Spotify & Twitch integration.

Download v2.0.6

1 Setup Spotify API

Create an app in the Spotify Dev Dashboard. Add http://127.0.0.1:8888/callback as the Redirect URI.

2 Launch & Link

Open the app, paste your keys, and click the authorize buttons.

3 Auto-Announce

Every time a new song starts, the app sends a message to your Twitch chat once.

4 Managing Updates

You can choose to Download, Skip, or Install Later when a new version is released.

5 Add to OBS

Use http://127.0.0.1:8888/overlay as a Browser Source (400x150).
